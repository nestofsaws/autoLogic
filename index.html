At Auto Logic, our ASE certified master technicians are paid a salary. Service advisors are also ASE certified technicians and they too are paid a salary.  Since we opened the door in 1985 this is the way we have done it. Our self evaluation isn't done by looking at the bottom line of a profit and loss statement, but is centered on the relationship we have with our customers. That relationship is built on uncompromising honesty, technical excellence, and kept promises. Here are some of the promises we make to you:

  • No surprises! You will get an estimate. It will be accurate. 
  • Trust! We will earn it. We will continually prove we are worthy of it.
  • Technical excellence! Knowledge and skill properly applied. No bozos, no beating on cars with the wrong tools, no bad ideas poorly applied to shorten the life of your automobile and drive the cost up. Proper diagnosis, proper repair.
  • We will care!! Just like you are a member of the family. We will take time to talk to you, explain your automobile and what it needs, and listen to what you are concerned about.
  • Honesty and Integrity! Our advice will be built on your needs not ours. We will never embrace a business model of maximum profit for minimum effort.

And that means, very simply, that you find your automobile to be as safe, reliable, lasting, and economical as is possible.

We know that if we do all these things well and consistently the profits will be a logical consequence. Since 1986 this philosophy has been applied to make Auto Logic consistently the most respected and acclaimed full service repair facility in the triangle.
